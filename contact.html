Process

A structured experience, designed to reduce noise and elevate outcome.

  1. Inquiry review
    We confirm date feasibility, venue constraints, guest profile, and scope alignment.
  2. Design consultation
    We define priorities, cultural considerations, and the design direction with clarity.
  3. Concept and proposal
    A cohesive concept with palette, staging approach, floral intent, layout logic, and investment range.
  4. Production planning
    Timeline, procurement, fabrication, and coordination are locked with accountability.
  5. Installation and reveal
    On-site execution with a hospitality mindset—clean finish, disciplined staging, seamless experience.
